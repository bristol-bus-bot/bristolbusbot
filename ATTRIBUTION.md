- **Bus Open Data Service (BODS)**, Department for Transport - timetables

- **Traveline National Dataset (TNDS)** - supplementary timetables for routes
  not published to BODS. https://www.travelinedata.org.uk/
- **NaPTAN** (National Public Transport Access Nodes) - bus stop locations and
  codes. OGL v3.0.
- **Bristol City Council, Bristol Bus Stops** - local stop-name and facility
  enrichment. Open Government Licence.
  https://www.data.gov.uk/dataset/bee75961-c3b8-4bcf-a41d-2ab7617e4793/bristol-bus-stops-raised-kerbs

  OGL v3.0; contains OS data © Crown copyright and database right.
- **bustimes.org** - fleet data (vehicle models, liveries, registrations),
  community-maintained. Used at low volume with local caching; displayed with
  attribution and links back.
- **Bluesky public APIs** - bot post retrieval for display on the live site.
- **OpenWeather** - weather data used for commentary context (bot).
